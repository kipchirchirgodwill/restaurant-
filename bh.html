Karibu Restaurant Menu

Where Kenyan warmth meets Chinese flavor

Starters Description
Samosa Trio: Crispy pastry stuffed with spiced beef, chicken, or vegetables.
Spring Rolls: Golden rolls filled with cabbage, carrots, and glass noodles.
Mandazi Basket: Sweet, fluffy Kenyan doughnuts served warm.

Soups Description
Hot & Sour Soup: Tangy broth with tofu, mushrooms, and bamboo shoots.
Chicken Clear Soup: Light broth with chicken, ginger, and scallions.
Karibu Vegetable Soup: Local greens simmered with herbs and spices.

Main Courses Description
Nyama Choma Platter: Charcoal-grilled goat or beef, served with ugali and kachumbari.
Kung Pao Chicken: Stir-fried chicken with peanuts, chili, and vegetables.
Sweet & Sour Pork: Crispy pork tossed in tangy pineapple sauce.
Tilapia Fry: Whole tilapia deep-fried, served with sukuma wiki and ugali.
Vegetable Chow Mein: Stir-fried noodles with mixed vegetables.

Chef’s Specials Description
Ugali & Stir-Fry Fusion: Ugali cubes wok-tossed with soy sauce and vegetables.
Chapati Wraps: Soft chapati filled with stir-fried beef and peppers.
Karibu Fried Rice: Rice with peas, carrots, eggs, and a touch of Kenyan spice.

Desserts Description
Chinese Almond Cookies: Crunchy, nutty treats.
Mango Pudding: Smooth pudding made with fresh Kenyan mangoes.
Coconut Mahamri: Sweet Kenyan dough infused with coconut.

Drinks Description
Fresh Sugarcane Juice: Refreshingly sweet juice from fresh sugarcane.
Masala Chai: Kenyan spiced tea brewed to perfection.
Fresh Tropical Juices: Assorted tropical juices (passion, mango, pineapple).
